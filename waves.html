Information about the brain and waves.

Alpha Waves

8–12 Hz

Relaxed but awake brain activity.

Beta Waves

13–30 Hz

Active thinking and concentration.

Theta Waves

4–8 Hz

Deep relaxation and meditation.

Delta Waves

0.5–4 Hz

Deep sleep brain activity.

Gamma Waves

30+ Hz

High-level cognitive processing.
